Maria and Andrei chose a quiet morning in Bran instead of a crowded venue. The fog sat low in the valley, the light was diffused and forgiving, and every frame felt unhurried. We shot mostly on a 35mm and 85mm, leaning into honest, candid moments rather than staged poses.

The day moved between the guesthouse, a small chapel, and a clearing overlooking the peaks. No big crowd — just the people who mattered, and enough space to breathe.
